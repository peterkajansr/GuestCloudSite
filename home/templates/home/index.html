Development process

We develop software by using an agile methodology Scrum that iteratively delivers demanded features. Each iteration results in working software that is ready for deployment. Scrum technology enables our team to quickly develop first working version and respond to emerging requirements.

A customer defines requirements at the beginning of each iteration. These requirements are analyzed, implemented and tested by our team. At the end of the iteration, the implemented features are presented to customer who either approves them or suggests improvements. The process offers:

Flexibility

The requirements are predefined for short iterations in order to adjust software specifications according to current client needs after each iteration.

Customer involvement

Customer is part of the development process. He or she specifies the requirements and approves their fulfillment (User Acceptance Testing).
